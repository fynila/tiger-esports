Barcelona forward open to leaving if given letter of freedom

Memphis Depay’s future at Barcelona continues to be shrouded in uncertainty. The 28-year-old has been at the club for just a year but finds himself staring at an exit this summer. Depay joined the Blaugrana on a Bosman deal last summer and made a big financial sacrifice to secure a move to Camp Nou. He had a solid enough debut campaign in Spain, finishing as the club’s joint top-scorer with 13 goals.

However, the signing of Robert Lewandowski and Raphinha, and the contract renewal of Ousmane Dembele, have pushed Depay down the pecking order of things at Barça. Apart from the three players mentioned above, the Dutchman will also have to battle with Ansu Fati, Pierre-Emerick Aubameyang and Ferran Torres for a starting berth under Xavi Hernandez.
